# HowlFlow

HowlFlow is a mobile app designed to help people with ADHD build sustainable focus habits through gamification and a companion wolf spirit guide.

## Core Features

- **Focus Timer** — Pomodoro, deep work, and sprint modes
- **Brain Dump** — Capture thoughts quickly, process them later
- **Routines** — Build and track daily routines with guided steps
- **Wolf Den** — Your wolf companion grows with your progress
- **Achievements** — Unlock milestones without shame mechanics

## Philosophy

HowlFlow is built on anti-shame principles. There are no streaks that punish you for missing a day, no guilt-inducing notifications, and no comparison features. Your wolf is always happy to see you.
